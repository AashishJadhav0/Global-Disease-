Description

Influenza is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses that infect the nose, throat, and sometimes the lungs. It can cause mild to severe illness and sometimes lead to death. The best way to prevent flu is by getting a flu vaccine each year.

Global Statistics

Annual influenza epidemics result in about 3-5 million cases of severe illness and about 290,000-650,000 respiratory deaths worldwide.

Symptoms

Common Symptoms

  • Fever or feeling feverish/chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Muscle or body aches

Emergency Symptoms

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Persistent chest pain
  • Severe muscle pain
  • Seizures
  • Bluish lips or face
